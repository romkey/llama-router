    How It Works

    The registry cache acts as a pull-through proxy for the Ollama model registry. When a model is pulled:

    1. The first pull downloads from registry.ollama.ai and caches the blobs locally
    2. Subsequent pulls on other providers are served from the local cache at LAN speed

    Blobs are content-addressed (SHA256) and never expire. Manifests are cached for 10 days and re-fetched after that.
